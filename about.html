Our Story

It all began with a simple love for flowers—a fascination with how a single bloom can brighten a room, lift a heart, or mark a special moment. Our founder, Rebecca, spent years learning the art of floristry, exploring gardens, markets, and fields to understand the beauty hidden in every petal.

From those early days, our team grew, bringing together creative minds who see each bouquet as a story waiting to be told. At FlorMoon, we don’t just deliver flowers; we deliver moments. Moments of happiness, connection, and beauty. Each stem is chosen with care, arranged with skill, and sent with love, so that the story of your emotions blooms in the hearts of those you cherish.
